About Us

Mediplus was established in 1986 with a focus on developing and delivering innovations that put the patient at the heart of everything we do.

The company has developed numerous world firsts in single use medical devices in Anaesthesia, Gynaecology and Urology, winning multiple awards for innovation. Our greatest achievement, winning two Queen's Awards; in 2013 for International Trade and in 2017 for Innovation.

Mediplus manufacture many of the products in the UK and sells them across the world to help hundreds of thousands of patients each year.
